Cash Connecta — Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

Cash Connecta (the "App") respects your privacy. This page explains what account-related data the App requests and how it is used.

Account access (android.permission.GET_ACCOUNTS)

The App requests android.permission.GET_ACCOUNTS only to enable authentication and link a device account (for example, Google Sign-In) to your app profile. The permission is used solely for sign-in/authentication and account selection dialogs when required.

We do not collect, sell, or share your account credentials or account lists with third parties. Account information is used only to authenticate and associate an account with your app data.

Data storage and sharing

The App does not store account credentials locally or on our servers. Any tokens used for authentication are handled by the authentication provider (e.g., Google) and follow their policies.

User controls

You may revoke account access at any time on your device via Settings → Accounts, or by removing app permissions in the device app settings.
